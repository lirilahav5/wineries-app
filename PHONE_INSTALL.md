# 📱 How to Install wineME App on Your Phone

## Step 1: Get Your Netlify URL

1. Go to https://app.netlify.com
2. Login to your account
3. You should see your site listed
4. **Copy the URL** - it looks like: `https://your-site-name.netlify.app`

## Step 2: Install on Your Phone

### For Android (Chrome Browser):

1. **Open Chrome** on your Android phone
2. **Type your Netlify URL** in the address bar (e.g., `https://your-site-name.netlify.app`)
3. **Wait for the page to load completely**
4. **Tap the menu button** (3 dots) in the top-right corner
5. **Look for "Add to Home screen"** or "Install app"
6. **Tap it**
7. **Confirm** by tapping "Add" or "Install"
8. **Done!** The app icon will appear on your home screen

### For iPhone (Safari Browser):

1. **Open Safari** on your iPhone
2. **Type your Netlify URL** in the address bar
3. **Wait for the page to load completely**
4. **Tap the Share button** (square with arrow pointing up) at the bottom
5. **Scroll down** and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
6. **Edit the name** if you want (or leave it as "wineME")
7. **Tap "Add"** in the top-right corner
8. **Done!** The app icon will appear on your home screen

## Troubleshooting

### If "Add to Home Screen" doesn't appear:

**Android:**
- Make sure you're using **Chrome** browser (not Firefox or others)
- Try opening the site in **Incognito mode** first, then normal mode
- Clear browser cache and try again

**iPhone:**
- Make sure you're using **Safari** (not Chrome)
- Check that the site loaded completely
- Try refreshing the page

### If the app doesn't work after installing:

1. Make sure you have internet connection
2. The app needs location permissions - allow when prompted
3. Try uninstalling and reinstalling

## Testing the Installation

After installing:
1. Tap the app icon on your home screen
2. It should open like a native app (no browser bar)
3. Test the features: map, list, search, etc.
