Management App (management-app)

This is the logo admins see when they download the management app

Location: management-app/public/logo.svg

How to View on Your Phone:

These logos appear as app icons on your phone's home screen after you install the apps. They should be visible in your app drawer or home screen.

If you can't see them:

  • Check your phone's home screen or app drawer
  • The icons should show a wine bottle with "wineME" text
  • Main app: Red background
  • Management app: Dark blue background
